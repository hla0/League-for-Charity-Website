About the Event


All proceeds go towards Alexandria House, our beneficiary.

"Founded in 1996, Alexandria House is a non-profit transitional residence and house of hospitality providing safe and supportive housing for women and children in the process of moving from emergency shelter to permanent housing... Alexandria House also serves its broader neighborhood community by providing educational and enrichment opportunities for residents and neighbors alike... The goal of Alexandria House is to be a community-oriented transitional house and neighborhood center which is intentionally multicultural, antiracist and where the needs of women who are economically poor are central." More information can be found at http://alexandriahouse.org/, but now you're probably wondering how YOU can help!


This year we are hosting our SECOND annual League for Charity Tournament. The tournament is allowed for anyone to enter and it will be played entirely online. Each week, teams of five will have to challenge an opposing team in a match to seal their spot in the coveted next round. The last three teams standing will win cash prizes of ($100, $75, $25), products valued up to $300. Entry fee is $5 per person ($25 per team) and all proceeds raised from this event will go directly to our local beneficiary Alexandria House, a Los Angeles based transitional home for women and children in the area.

FAQ


Do I have to be level 30?

If you are registering as a team, it is not required for you to be level 30. However, if you are registering as a single player, we would like for you to be level 30 or we cannot guarantee you a spot on a team.

Can we pay in person?

Unless you are a UCLA student, no. This year we will be accepting Venmo though.

Are substitutes allowed to come in during the game if someone is unable to reconnect?

No, connectivity issues are at the players discretion. We will only reward rematches if the Riot servers are responsible for the connectivity issue.

Will phone numbers of the game officials be provided for them to call during the time of the match?

E-mail is the best way to contact game officials, they will all be checking their e-mails rigorously.

Will the match-ups be random or by stats?

They will be random.

How will we be receiving our prizes?

We will mail you your prizes.

How will I be paired up with other players if I registered as a single player?

If you register as a single player you will be randomly placed into a team with other single players.

What do we do when we can not reach our opposing team to arrange game information?

If there is any problem contacting the opposing team, please email us at ckilolforcharity@gmail.com. If the team does not respond by the end of the week, your team will win by default.

Will a game bracket be uploaded on the website or Facebook page?

They will be uploaded on both.
